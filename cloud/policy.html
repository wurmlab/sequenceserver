Privacy policy

We take your privacy very seriously.

Contact information: We keep your contact information only for login and billing purposes. We will not share your contact information with anyone.

Databases: We similarly take the privacy of your data seriously. What you put in your SequenceServer instances is only yours – only the users that the administrator of your instance authorises to access the data can perform searches on your data. No other users can access it. The data are stored on encrypted disks under tight security control.

When you delete your databases, they are permanently erased from our servers. If you close your account, your data are similarly erased from our servers.

Search results: When you perform a search, the search results are accessible and stored at a stable url (e.g., http://yourinstance.sequenceserver.com/afs3-sd93-uu3i-s3j1). This address is only accessible to authenticated users of your particular instance (we will in the future implement a sharing mechanism that enables users to share results with non-authenticated users in a controlled manner). The search results are retained on the server for 30 days, and are then permanently deleted.

Infrastructure: We have a strong security track record, and use multiple layers of security to prevent unwanted and unintentional access to your databases and searches. Authorized SequenceServer engineers can access your data on occasion, but only to set up your databases, or to help troubleshoot a problem that may come up as part of your support request.

Refund policy

We have a no-questions-asked 30 day money-back guarantee.

We will refund everything if you cancel in the first 30 days. If you cancel after the first 30 days, your subscription will terminate at the end of your current billing period (month or year).

There are no cancellation fees or charges. We will refund you the whole amount if you cancel in the first 30 days. If you cancel after the first 30 days, your subscription will simply not renew at the end of your current billing period.
